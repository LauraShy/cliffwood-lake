Our Community

Welcome to Cliffwood Lake, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Stockholm, New Jersey. Our close-knit community is known for its warm, welcoming spirit and scenic charm. With a rich tradition of hosting events for both kids and adults throughout the year, Cliffwood Lake brings people together in meaningful ways. Centered around a beautiful 24-acre lake, residents and visitors enjoy peaceful days by the waterfront, with opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and socializing.
