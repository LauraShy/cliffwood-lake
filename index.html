Our Community

Welcome to Cliffwood Lake, a hidden gem nestled in the heart of Stockholm, New Jersey. Our close-knit community is known for its warm, welcoming spirit and scenic charm. With a rich tradition of hosting events for both kids and adults throughout the year, Cliffwood Lake brings people together in meaningful ways. Centered around a beautiful 24-acre lake, residents enjoy peaceful days by the waterfront, with opportunities for swimming, sunbathing, and socializing.

Our community ballfield, complete with playsets and other fun amenities for kids, is the perfect spot for games, picnics, and outdoor fun for people of all ages.
